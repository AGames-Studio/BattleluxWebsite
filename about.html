About Battlelux

Experience the next generation of realistic battles developed by a small international studio with a big vision.

What is Battlelux?

Battlelux is a tactical third-person shooter with a focus on realism, team play, and creative freedom. Whether you're fighting for survival in the fast-paced Battle Royale or delving deep into the story in the campaign, Battlelux offers excitement, style, and strategic depth.

Create your own character, choose your playstyle, and battle against players from all over the world on a variety of impressive maps.

About AGames Studio

AGames Studio is a small, independent development studio based in the USA with team members, supporters, and creative minds from all over the world. We share a passion for games that are not only technically powerful, but also emotionally engaging and exciting.

Our goal is to create memorable experiences. Battlelux is our biggest passion project to date and we are constantly developing it further together with our community.

System requirements

  • Operating System: Windows 10 / macOS 11 / Linux
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-8100 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti or AMD Radeon RX 570
  • Storage Space: 10 GB
  • Recommended: SSD for fast loading times

Timeline

2023 – First Concept Phase

Battlelux begins as a passion project for a small development team.

Spring 2024 – Internal Prototype

The first playable versions are tested internally and in a small group.

Summer 2024 – Community Alpha

Battlelux becomes playable for early backers feedback flows directly into development.

2025 – Public Beta & Launch

Official beta version for everyone. The final release is planned for the end of 2025.
