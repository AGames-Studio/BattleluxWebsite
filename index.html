Battlelux

Battlelux is a realistic and thrilling third person shooter game. You can create your own character and customize your weapons to suit your style. Whether you prefer guns or melee combat, you can defeat your enemies in various ways. Join the Battle Royale mode and survive until you are the last one standing. Or dive into the story mode and enjoy an engaging plot and stunning graphics.

Customizable Weapons

Make your weapon your own with endless customization options! Purchase new items to enhance your weapon’s performance and appearance! Add flair and personality to your weapon with cool accessories!

Fighting Styles

Unleash your ‘Fighting Style’ in battle! Find fight style masters on the map and learn a fighting style from them! Train with them to perfect your fighting style.
